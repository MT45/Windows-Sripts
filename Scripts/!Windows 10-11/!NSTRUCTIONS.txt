Command to list all power configs (intresting if you want to make desktop pcfg shortcut, eg. make a shortcut to lower your CPU's clock to save energy or reduce temps (and fan speed) while doing hw): See image "desktop pcfg shortcut"

How to set max/min frequency of the cpu in powercfg:
1.	Open Control Panel\Hardware and Sound\Power Options
2.	Change Plan Settings
3.	change advanced power settings
4.	Processor Power Managment
5.	Change settings in "Maximum processor state" & "Minimum processor state"
6.	My example see image "This settings block my cpu clock at 3Ghz (good enought for hw)"
